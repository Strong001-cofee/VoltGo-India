VoltGo India revolutionizes electric vehicle management by creating an intelligent ecosystem that connects you with the nearest charging infrastructure. When you open the app, it automatically requests your location permission and uses GPS to pinpoint your exact coordinates. Based on this data, our sophisticated algorithm calculates distances to nearby charging stations and displays them in order of proximity.
