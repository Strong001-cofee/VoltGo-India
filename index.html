SMART EV ECOSYSTEM

VoltGo India revolutionizes electric vehicle management by creating an intelligent ecosystem that connects you with the nearest charging infrastructure. Our advanced geolocation technology instantly identifies your position and displays real-time information about charging stations in your vicinity.

When you open the app, it automatically requests your location permission and uses GPS to pinpoint your exact coordinates. Based on this data, our sophisticated algorithm calculates distances to nearby charging stations and displays them in order of proximity, complete with availability status, charging speeds, and estimated wait times.

Whether you're planning a long journey or need an emergency charge, VoltGo India ensures you're never stranded. Pre-book your EV, reserve charging slots, and manage your entire electric mobility experience from one powerful platform.
